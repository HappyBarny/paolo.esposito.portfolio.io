Introduction - Working in progress

Designing a user-friendly and effective dating app presents a range of unique challenges in the field of UX design, especially when competing in a market saturated with platforms like Tinder, Badoo, and Hinge. Each of these apps has its own approach to fostering connections, yet there remains untapped potential for improving how users interact and form meaningful relationships online.
For this project, I collaborated with Rossana Donofrio to deliver a fresh, innovative, and research-driven approach to the dating app experience, aiming to create the best possible solution for users. What sets our dating app apart is its focus on deeper compatibility and authentic interactions, going beyond the surface-level swiping that apps like Tinder and Badoo rely on.
